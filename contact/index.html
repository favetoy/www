Frequently Asked Questions

❓How do you select products for FaveToy?

We carefully curate each product based on innovation, educational value, safety certifications, and user reviews. Every toy must meet our high standards for quality and inspire learning through play.

🛒How do purchases work?

When you click on a product, you'll be directed to Amazon.com where you can complete your purchase. We earn a small commission from qualifying purchases, which helps us maintain and improve our curation service.

🚚What about shipping and returns?

All shipping and returns are handled directly by Amazon according to their policies. Most items qualify for Prime shipping, and Amazon's customer service team handles any issues with orders.

🎯Can you recommend toys for specific ages?

Absolutely! Send us an email with your child's age, interests, and any specific learning goals. We'll provide personalized recommendations from our curated collection.

🏢Do you work with schools or educators?

Yes! We love partnering with educators to bring innovative STEM tools into classrooms. Contact us for bulk recommendations and educational resources.

🌟How often do you add new products?

We continuously research and test new tech toys, adding the best innovations to our collection weekly. Sign up for our newsletter to stay updated on the latest additions.
